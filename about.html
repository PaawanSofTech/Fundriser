What Makes Us Unique

It’s safe and transparent

All the products that you donate towards are delivered to the NGOs by us. There are no monetary transactions with the NGOs - you know exactly what you’re donating and where it’s going.

You receive regular updates

Your experience before, during, and after making a donation is extremely important to us. Hence, you will receive regular updates from the campaign you’ve donated to about the delivery, distribution, and usage of the products.

We care

If you have any feedback, thoughts, or concerns, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team. We’re dedicated to making this the best and most seamless experience for you - so we’re all ears.
